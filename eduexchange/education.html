Educational Resources for Buying & Selling Stocks

Welcome to Edustock's educational page! Here you will find helpful resources to learn more about how the stock market works, buying and selling stocks, and other investment strategies. These resources are perfect for both beginners and those looking to sharpen their skills.

  • Investopedia: How to Start Investing in Stocks

    A beginner-friendly guide that explains the basics of investing in stocks, including terminology and strategies.

  • The Balance: Stock Trading Tips for Beginners

    Learn the essentials of buying and selling stocks, as well as some important tips to get started.

  • NerdWallet: What Is the Stock Market and How Does It Work?

    Understand how the stock market functions, the role it plays in the economy, and why investing can be beneficial.

  • Robinhood: How Does the Stock Market Work?

    Get insights into how the stock market operates and what you need to know as an investor.

  • U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission: Investor Tips

    A PDF guide providing essential tips and best practices for making informed decisions as an investor.
